Site Clean-Up and Miscellaneous Works

Canal specializes in the construction of various types of telecommunication towers, catering to the diverse needs of the industry. From traditional lattice towers to modern monopoles and stealth structures, we offer a comprehensive range of construction solutions tailored to our clients' specific requirements. Our experienced team ensures that each tower is built to the highest standards of quality and safety, meeting all regulatory and industry standards. With a proven track record of successful projects, we are committed to delivering reliable and cost-effective tower construction services.

Versatility in Misc Works

Beyond our core services, we excel in handling Miscellaneous (Misc) Works associated with GSM infrastructure. This includes a wide range of tasks, from equipment installations to site improvements and enhancements.

Custom Solutions
